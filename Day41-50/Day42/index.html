Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the Dough

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 1 large egg

For the Filling

  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine all the dough ingredients.
  2. Knead the dough on a floured surface until smooth.
  3. Let the dough rise in a warm place until doubled in size.
  4. Roll out the dough and spread the filling mixture over it.
  5. Roll the dough into a log and slice into individual rolls.
  6. Place the rolls in a baking dish and let them rise again.
  7. Bake in a preheated oven until golden brown.
